History & Vision

Pakistan Railways was established in 1861 and has grown to be a crucial part of the country's economy. It plays a vital role in transporting passengers and goods across the nation, contributing significantly to economic growth.

With modernization efforts and a vision for sustainable development, Pakistan Railways aims to improve efficiency, safety, and connectivity for a better travel experience.
