Find Answers to Your Questions

How can I book a ticket?

You can book a ticket online through the official Pakistan Railways website or visit your nearest railway station.

What are the available payment options?

You can pay using credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, or at the railway station.

Can I cancel my ticket?

Yes, you can cancel your ticket online or at the railway station, subject to cancellation policies.

Are there discounts for senior citizens?

Yes, senior citizens can avail of special discounts on ticket prices.

How can I check train schedules?

Train schedules are available on the official website and at railway stations.
